Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to generate more revenue for our company. We are well known locally and we are in a high travel area. People are coming in to spend their time here and our services can provide them something to do with their time while they are here. By taking the step online we can reach even more customers outside of our local area and further grow our business.

Audience

Who are the target customers? We hope to have families, friends and individuals come and experience our services. They are anywhere between 8 and 60 and can swim. What do they want? These people will have a desire to experience the thrilling and beauty of a white water rafting experience. How will our audience usually access my site? They will mainly be accessing our services via desktop.
